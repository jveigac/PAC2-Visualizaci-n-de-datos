Data Visualizations

1. Heatmap - Correlación Sectores con SP500

This heatmap visualizes the correlation between various sectors and the SP500 index. It provides insights into how different sectors perform concerning the overall market.

2. Voronoi - Altitud estaciones metereológicas

This Voronoi diagram displays the altitude at various meteorological stations, helping to understand how altitude varies across different regions.

3. Tiles Grid Map - Presión fiscal en paises europeos

This tiles grid map shows the fiscal pressure in various European countries, offering a comparative view of economic conditions.
